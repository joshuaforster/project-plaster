# How Much Does It Cost to Renovate a House in the Leicester, Rutland or East Midlands?

Renovating a home is one of the biggest financial and emotional decisions most homeowners will ever make and one of the first questions we always get is:

> “How much will we be looking to spend?”

As with most things, it always depends.

In this blog, we have provided you with a breakdown of the typical costs, what affects the price and how proper renovation management can save you time, money and stress.

---

## What Affects Renovation Costs?

Every project is different, but the main factors that influence cost are:

- **Size and scope of the work** (e.g. loft vs. whole house)  
- **Structural changes** (moving walls = more cost)  
- **Plumbing & electrics** (especially in older homes)  
- **Finish level** – high-end fixtures vs. standard  
- **Planning permissions and architectural involvement**

---

## Typical Ballpark Costs (East Midlands)

Here’s a rough guide to average renovation costs in Leicestershire and Rutland, based on a standard 3–4 bed home in 2024:

- **Full House Renovation:** £75,000 – £180,000+  
- **Kitchen Renovation:** £15,000 – £45,000  
- **Bathroom Renovation:** £6,000 – £15,000  
- **Extension (20–30m²):** £35,000 – £65,000+  
- **Loft Conversion:** £30,000 – £50,000  

*Note: These are average ranges and actual costs may vary depending on materials, access, design, and trades availability.*
